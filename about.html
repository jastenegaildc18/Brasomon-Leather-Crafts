Our Story

Over the course of year, we continue to practice the art of leathercrafting. Making leather into craft objects, using shaping techniques and coloring techniques are quiet enjoyable. It started as a hobby, until lots of people begin to admire the art of leather crafts, that soon launched the Brasomon Leather Crafts.

We know that spreading happiness in a simple way of appreciation requires passion, creativity, and a dedication of what you do with a sincere heart.

Quality is our Focus



For the past year, our goal has been exceed customer expectations. It is our tradition and pride of serving the customers. From a wide selection of leather, tools used, and care, Brasomon Leather Crafts has been a choice for craft-lover people out there. From thoughtfully designing each product, we focused on quality which entrusted us to be by you.

By cultivating close relationship with our customers, we are able to give them a high quality products that offers performance and value. We have always taken our pride in our craftsmanship and only use the finest materials to bring you quality products at a reasonable price.

Our team works diligently to ensure every product with Brasomon Leather Crafts name meets the highest quality standards you expect. We have deep knowledge of leather and are committed to putting our experience to work for you.
